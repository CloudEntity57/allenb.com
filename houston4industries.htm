Seventy-seven foreign governments have representation in Houston through consular offices. Many also maintain trade offices here.
Houston consistently ranks as one of the top five cities in the nation in the number of consular offices.
Forty international chambers of commerce and trade associations have established themselves in Houston. Houston's foreign business and international community are also supported by more than 80 private business and cultural organizations.
Houston has 14 sister-city relationships promoting business and investment opportunities across four continents: Australia (1), Asia (5), Europe (7) and South America (1).
Twenty-two foreign banks representing 11 nations operate in Houston and provide financial assistance to the international community.
