Company Profile  

        In 1986, the year of Houston's sesquicentennial, Allen Brothers, REALTORS came into existence.  The Allen brothers, the founders of Houston, were Houston's original real estate brokers.

    In 1988 we moved our office to its present location, 1828 Bissonnet at Woodhead, just north of Rice University in the museum area. Our company  takes full advantage of the latest technology.  We view our work in real estate, serving our customers, as an opportunity to express the highest and best that is in us. Our company  places great importance on responding to our clients, making each transaction unique, since individual clients require different amounts of information and education depending on their real estate histories.   

Areas of expertise include residential and commercial real estate transactions.  David Bryan, CCIM, offers individuals the opportunity to join others in purchasing managed real estate, with limited liability, often outperforming many other kinds of investments and often with the potential for major appreciation.
