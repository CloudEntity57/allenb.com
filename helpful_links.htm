Texas Real Estate Center:     http://recenter.tamu.edu/   

The Center is the nation's largest publicly funded organization devoted to real estate research. Most of our $2 million in annual funding comes from real estate license fees paid by more than 100,000 professionals. A nine-member advisory committee appointed by the governor provides research guidance and approves the budget.
